About Kristijan

Kristijan is a Still Photographer

Kristijan Bančić is a Croatian photographer from Rovinj. He seriously encountered photography in high school when he chose a photographic direction, shortly afterward he enrolled in graphic design in Zagreb. He is a student of photography in Ljubljana at VIST the Faculty of Applied Sciences. Best known for his work on Ekho (2011) as Still Photographer and various other projects. He worked in the Museum city of Rovinj for four years as a photographic archivist - negative asset manager. His work was exhibited at several group exhibitions: Cyprus, Leonberg, Kumanovo, Ljubljana, and Rovinj. He won the FIAP diploma 2011 for Croatia in open section - colour, for Distorted Reality in Kumanovo.

Kristijan Bančić is a member of Center of Visual Arts - Cvu-Batana in Rovinj since 2011.

He most likes to shoot abstract photography.
