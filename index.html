What can I shoot for you?

Creating Film Still Images

Over the past several years, I've been working on student films as a freelance motion picture still photographer and various other experimental projects in the multimedia field, as my portfolio will attest. I have over a decade of experience in photography. I try to incorporate different photographic methods to create something exceptional, even in dim light, knowing that there is light; I can still photograph. Always respecting the fundamental rules of lighting and composition elements in the scene. I am passionate about finding the ultimate diverse angle relative to the subject, and seizing decisive moments in a split second that work best and resonate with the authentic film’s identity. My priority objective is to capture visual notes and document the keyframes and final fragments of the entire filmmaking process, from start to finish, that occurs on the film set; utilizing the optimal available tools to achieve successful shooting for publicity development and promotion purposes of the film in any way possible. To tell the narrative from my own unique cinematic perspective.

Let's talk about what I can do for you. Let me create something great for your movie! I am always open and ready for new challenges and opportunities in the area of photography. I am free and flexible to travel to different locations and adapt easily to different working cultures. I speak the following languages: English, Italian, Slovenian and Croatian. I collaborate effectively within interdisciplinary international teams.

I'm also active on social media so if you're curious about what I'm up to you can check my work on IMDb.
